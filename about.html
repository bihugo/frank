LOCATION OF BUGEMA UNIVERSITY

The university is on land that measures 640 acres (1.00 sq mi), in Kalagala sub-county, Bamunanika county, Luweero District in the Central Region of Uganda.
The main campus is approximately 33 kilometres (21 mi), by road, northeast of Kampala, Uganda's capital and largest city.
